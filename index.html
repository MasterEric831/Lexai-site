What's the difference between an LLC and a C-Corp?
Great question. A C-Corp is taxed separately from its owners and is preferred for venture-backed startups due to equity flexibility. An LLC offers pass-through taxation and less overhead — better for solo founders or small teams.

What state should I incorporate in?
Delaware is the gold standard for C-Corps planning to raise VC funding. Nevada and Wyoming are popular for LLCs seeking strong privacy protections. For most solo founders, your home state is perfectly fine.
